Verdant Engagement Platform

Build custom solutions from modular, extensible, tools.

Modular and extensible

VEP solutions are built on a robust library of shared modules, so everyone benefits.

You pay for the modules you need, and incorporate custom code if and when you need it to deliver only the absolute best experiences for your customers and constituents.

Bespoke

The platform is built to power applications built to your exact specifications. That means no hacks, no workarounds, no trying to make a tool meant for a blog work for your app.

Built on Open Source technologies

The platform is built using cutting edge open source technologies, to deliver the very best performance and the very best stability.

Transparent Pricing

The Verdant Engagement Platform is built to give you no surprise pricing. That means we will work with you before your application is built to understand how it is going to be used, and what kind of pricing makes sense.

This company was founded in part because we saw state governments buying seat licenses for an application that was open for public registration. Verdant Technology wants you to have a good experience, not just your customers.
